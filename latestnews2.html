Across Government Panel Contract for the delivery of non accredited training to NTG

Published 24/11/2017

EASA-CORP is pleased to have been selected as a member of the Across Government Panel Contract for the delivery of non-accredited Capability and Leadership Framework (CLF) training to the Northern Territory Government (D16-0047) for the period October 2017 – October 2020

Please find below a list of training topics EASA-CORP can deliver to your departments in Darwin, Alice Springs, Katherine, Tennant Creek and Nhulunbuy:

  • Business Acumen Developing Self
  • Social Awareness and Community Engagement
  • Working in the NTPS
  • Building Management Skills

As an NTG Employee Assistance Program provider, EASA-CORP can also assist with a variety of psychological and organisational services, including Counselling, Return-to-Work Assessments, Workplace Investigations, Mediations, Group Facilitated Discussions and Coaching.


Should you have any queries regarding the NTG Panel Contract training, or any of EASA-CORP’s other services, please do not hesitate to get in touch, we are available to meet with you in person or over the phone to discuss any training needs further.
